Our Story

A legacy of love, carried forward.

The ASG Foundation was created to honour the legacy of Alban Sekeso Gundu, a man remembered for his compassion, humility, and unwavering commitment to helping others. ASG stands for his name, and through it, we carry his values and heart for service.

We are a non-profit organisation dedicated to supporting orphans (ages 0–18), widows, and children with disabilities in Zimbabwe. Through compassionate, hands-on engagement, we strive to restore dignity, meet urgent needs, and inspire hope in underserved communities.

We work in close partnership with local foundations across Zimbabwe to identify those in greatest need and respond with tangible support. Whether you're here to give, learn, or partner with us, thank you for being part of this mission.

Meet the Team

The people behind the mission.

A passionate group of individuals dedicated to creating meaningful impact through compassion, collaboration, and community service.

Rudo Gundu

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Rudo founded ASG Foundation in loving memory of her late father, Alban Sekeso Gundu. Based in Atlanta, Georgia, she brings a global perspective and deep sense of purpose to her work, and has also contributed to Kurera Children's Trust in Zimbabwe. She works for a software company empowering non-profits through technology, an alignment that echoes her own commitment to service.

Jordash Manyangadze

Administrative Manager

A Finance and Accounting student based in South Africa, originally from Zimbabwe. Jordash leads awareness and outreach campaigns, handles administrative tasks, and supports marketing efforts. Passionate about financial literacy and community development, he is dedicated to making a meaningful difference both behind the scenes and on the ground.

Tsitsi Chingoka

Director of Finance & Marketing

A purpose-driven marketing and business development professional with over 10 years of experience across pharmaceutical, FMCG, and NGO sectors. Tsitsi holds a BSc Honours in Marketing, an MBA, and diplomas in Project Management and Digital Marketing. She brings deep compassion, integrity, and social responsibility to everything she does.
